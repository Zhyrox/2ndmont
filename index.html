Our Story

August 7, 2025

First Meet

How easily we could have missed each other forever.

To meet the way we did, on a website like Omegle, feels impossible. It is a place built for things that do not last, an endless hallway of doors opening and closing, where people appear for a second and then disappear into the dark with a single click. I went online expecting nothing, fully used to being completely alone in that empty digital space.

But when your screen appeared, the cold, random luck of the world suddenly stopped.

It makes no sense that a love like ours could start in a place meant only for short, passing moments. We were just two strangers lost in a giant maze, and against all odds, we found each other. I never expected that a random choice to log on that night would lead me out of my lonely world and straight to you. What started as a total accident became the very thing that keeps me grounded.

January 1, 2026

The First "I Love You"

The new year arrives not with the striking of a clock, but with the quiet, terrifying realization that the trial has ended, and the verdict is entirely merciful.

For so long, my courtship felt like pacing through the endless, cold corridors of an unyielding bureaucracy, submitting petitions to an invisible court, fully prepared for the standard, devastating silence. I did not expect an answer. I certainly did not expect the heavens to open on the very first day of the year.

When those words, your first "I love you", finally left your lips, it was as if a law of the universe had suddenly shifted in my favor. The heavy, bureaucratic weight of my longing was instantly dissolved. To hear that from you was not just an answer; it was a sudden, breathtaking acquittal from the solitude I had grown so accustomed to. You have rewritten the entire architecture of my world, and for the first time, the world is profoundly, overwhelmingly beautiful.

Today

Two Months Strong

Happy second monthsary, my love. Today is finally the day.

This text is my only gift to you, and I truly hope you like it. It is the only thing I have to give right now, because my pockets are completely empty and I am broke at the moment.

But even in this cold, empty room where I have nothing of value to offer, my feelings for you are the one thing that remains completely real and solid. In a world where everything feels heavy, complicated, and impossible to control, loving you is the only simple truth I have. I love you, my love, with everything that I am.

The smell of lavender

It makes no sense, but the scent of lavender always brings me straight back to you. It is a strange trick of the mind, because you never mentioned the flower itself, only that you loved the color purple.

Yet, in the quiet spaces of my day, that smell acts like a sudden command I cannot ignore. In a world that often feels cold and random, this tiny connection is the one thing that instantly breaks through the grayness and fills my thoughts with you.

Mango cake

I spent my whole life hating cakes, refusing to see any value in them. But then you handed me that mango cake, and with a single gesture, you broke a rule I had kept for years.

Now, whenever the world feels heavy and I am at my lowest, it is the only thing I crave. It is a strange, beautiful contradiction, that in my darkest moments, the only thing that can comfort me is the very thing I used to reject, simply because it came from your hands.
